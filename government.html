Who We Serve

Built for Public Procurement

Public agencies need suppliers who understand how procurement actually works — firm pricing, clear invoices, dependable delivery windows, and the certifications that keep a bid compliant. Lights On America is built for exactly that.

As a certified Veteran Owned Small Business, we help agencies meet supplier-diversity and veteran set-aside goals while delivering competitive, spec-matched lighting and electrical supply. We carry the bid through end-to-end — sourcing, pricing, compliance documentation, and coordinated delivery.

We fulfill through established manufacturer and distributor relationships, which means we can supply contract quantities without you carrying the risk of a single-source vendor.

How We Work

From Solicitation to Delivery

01

Solicitation Review

We read the ITB/RFP closely — line items, certifications, delivery terms, and addenda — and confirm we can meet every requirement.

02

Sourcing & Pricing

We obtain spec-matched supplier quotes, confirm new-OEM condition and lead times, and build firm, all-in delivered pricing.

03

Compliant Bid

We assemble the full response — pricing forms, affidavits, references, and acknowledgments — in the agency's required format.

04

Fulfillment

On award, we coordinate distributor drop-ship to meet delivery windows, with detailed packing slips and responsive account support.
