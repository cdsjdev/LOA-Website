Service 01

Energy-Efficient Lighting

Lights On America designs and deploys advanced LED and smart lighting systems that dramatically reduce energy consumption while delivering superior illumination quality. Our lighting solutions are engineered for long-term performance — cutting operational costs and environmental impact simultaneously.

From warehouses and manufacturing floors to campuses and government facilities, our systems are customized to meet each client's exact requirements — including integration with building management systems and automated controls.

  • Commercial LED retrofits
  • Industrial high-bay lighting
  • Smart lighting controls
  • Emergency & safety lighting
  • Outdoor & perimeter lighting
  • Campus-wide installations
  • Energy auditing & analysis
  • BMS/SCADA integration

Service 02

Charging Stations & Transformers

As the electrification of transportation accelerates, Lights On America delivers state-of-the-art EV charging infrastructure and industrial transformer solutions built for reliability, scalability, and modern grid compatibility.

Our transformer expertise spans decades — we design, source, and install high-performance transformers for industrial and commercial applications where reliability is non-negotiable. Combined with our EV charging capabilities, we offer a complete electrical infrastructure solution.

  • Level 2 EV charging stations
  • DC fast charging (DCFC)
  • Fleet charging solutions
  • Charging network management
  • Distribution transformers
  • Pad-mounted transformers
  • Power conditioning units
  • Grid integration services
