Forty-Five Years of American Innovation

From our founding to today, Lights On America has remained at the forefront of energy and technology — driven by a commitment to excellence, integrity, and service.

Our Story

Built on a Foundation of Service

Lights On America was founded with a singular vision: to bring innovation, discipline, and excellence to the American energy and technology marketplace. For over forty-five years, that vision has guided every product we design and every solution we deliver.

What began as a focused operation has grown into a nationally recognized company with coast-to-coast reach, serving clients from independent businesses to major universities and government contractors.

Our central location gives us a decisive logistical advantage — we reach more than fifty percent of the United States market and sixty percent of the Canadian market, delivering with a speed and reliability that larger, less agile companies simply cannot match.

We pride ourselves on our unique approach to solving complicated problems, specializing in creating cutting-edge solutions that precisely meet the needs of our clients.

Certification

Veteran Owned Small Business

Lights On America is a certified Veteran Owned Small Business (VOSB), recognized by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs. This certification reflects our founder's extraordinary military career and our ongoing commitment to American values.

As a VOSB, we are eligible for set-aside federal contracts and qualify under numerous state and local supplier diversity programs — providing additional value to clients seeking certified partners.

Verified by the CVE

This seal is issued by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs Center for Verification and Evaluation (CVE) — confirming Lights On America as an officially certified Veteran Owned Small Business, eligible for federal set-aside contracts and supplier diversity programs nationwide.
