Who We Are

A Legacy of Innovation & American Excellence

For over forty-five years, Lights On America has been an innovative leader — designing computer software and hardware, state-of-the-art charging stations and transformers, energy-efficient lighting, and alternative sources of energy.

Centrally located to serve more than fifty percent of the United States market and sixty percent of the Canadian market, we deliver fast turnaround that eliminates production downtime and enhances productivity.
