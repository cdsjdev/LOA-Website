About Matthew Drozd

Matthew Drozd is the founder and president of Lights On America and The Drozd Group Inc. — a leader whose career spans extraordinary military service, national-level government work, and decades of private-sector innovation.

A highly decorated officer, Matt served with distinction in both the United States Army and the United States Air Force, achieving the rank of Lieutenant Colonel. His military career was defined by leadership at the highest levels of American government and defense.

Pentagon Service — September 11, 2001

One of the defining chapters of Matt's service came during his time at the Pentagon, where he was present on September 11, 2001. During this period, he served three different Chairmen of the Joint Chiefs of Staff — the nation's highest-ranking military officer — as well as the Secretary of Defense and the Secretary of State.

This experience — operating at the intersection of military strategy and national security at one of the most consequential moments in American history — instilled in Matt a sense of mission, resilience, and accountability that continues to define how Lights On America operates today.

Academic & Public Service

Beyond his military accomplishments, Matthew Drozd was one of the top development officers for Penn State University — one of the nation's leading research institutions. His fundraising and development work at Penn State demonstrated his ability to build relationships, secure resources, and deliver results in high-pressure environments.

His service and reputation were such that he was referred to serve as the Secretary of the Army — a testament to the esteem in which he was held by military and government leadership alike.

Founding Lights On America

Matt founded Lights On America with a mission to bring the same level of excellence, innovation, and accountability that defined his military career to the American energy and technology marketplace. Over forty-five years, his vision has shaped the company into a nationally recognized leader serving clients from prestigious universities to government agencies and Fortune 500 companies.

Under his leadership, Lights On America has built a reputation for solving complicated problems with creative, cutting-edge solutions — always delivering on time, always exceeding expectations.

The Organization Behind Lights On America

Lights On America operates as part of The Drozd Group Inc. — a Pittsburgh-based holding entity that reflects Matt Drozd's broader vision for American industry and innovation.
