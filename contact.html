Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What industries do you serve?

We serve industrial, commercial, government, educational, and healthcare clients across the U.S. and Canada. If you have a complex energy or technology challenge, we can help.

Are you eligible for government contracts?

Yes. As a certified Veteran Owned Small Business (VOSB), Lights On America qualifies for federal set-aside contracts and numerous state and local supplier diversity programs.

How quickly can you respond to a project inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within one business day. For urgent projects, call us directly at 412-364-1600 for immediate assistance.

Do you work on projects outside the United States?

Yes — our reach extends throughout Canada as well. Our west coast and east coast offices also position us to serve international clients with North American operations.

Can you handle large-scale or multi-site projects?

Absolutely. We have the operational capacity and logistics network to manage large, complex, multi-site deployments across North America with consistent quality.

What makes Lights On America different?

Our unique approach to solving complicated problems, our veteran-led discipline, and our 45+ years of experience mean clients receive solutions that truly work — not off-the-shelf products retrofitted to their needs.
