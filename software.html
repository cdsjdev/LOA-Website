Software for the Lighting Trade

Tools that take the friction out of quoting, bidding, and managing lighting product data — built by a team that runs real lighting bids every week.

The Problem

Bidding Lighting Is Still Done in Spreadsheets

Lighting distributors and contractors lose hours to manual work: rekeying RFP line items, chasing supplier quotes by email, matching cryptic model numbers across catalogs, and rebuilding the same pricing workbook for every bid.

Lights On America lives this workflow. We pair 45+ years in the lighting and electrical business with modern, AI-assisted software development — so we build tools that fit how the trade actually works, not generic software bent to fit.

What We Build

Purpose-Built Modules

📋

RFP & Bid Automation

Turn a solicitation into a structured, priceable bid in minutes instead of hours.

  • Parse ITB/RFP line items automatically
  • Track deadlines, addenda & questions
  • Generate compliant response packages
  • Reusable templates & affidavits
🧮

Quote Builder

Build delivered, margin-aware quotes from supplier costs with full transparency.

  • Markup-over-invoice pricing logic
  • Freight & delivery roll-ups
  • Multi-supplier cost comparison
  • Branded PDF quote output
🗂️

Catalog & SKU Matching

Resolve messy model numbers to real products across manufacturer catalogs.

  • Cross-reference equivalents (e.g. IZT/ITZ)
  • Normalize specs: wattage, CCT, base
  • Certification flags (UL/ETL/DLC)
  • Spot fluorescent-vs-LED mismatches
📧

Sourcing & RFQ Outreach

Request, track, and compare supplier quotes without living in your inbox.

  • Templated RFQ emails to suppliers
  • Reply tracking & quote capture
  • Lead-time & stock comparison
  • Deadline reminders
