Lighting Products & Supply

A reliable supply partner for commercial, industrial, and institutional lighting — LED tubes, lamps, ballasts, fixtures, and controls. Tell us what you need and we'll quote it.

What We Supply

Product Categories

We source new, OEM-quality lighting products from established manufacturers and distributors. We are not a retail storefront — we supply by quote, which lets us match exact specifications, certifications, and quantities to your project or contract.

💡

LED Tubes & Lamps

Energy-efficient replacements across the most common form factors.

  • T8 / T5 LED tubes (Type A, B, C)
  • Ballast-bypass & plug-and-play
  • HID & metal halide lamps
  • 2 ft / 3 ft / 4 ft / 8 ft lengths
🔧

Ballasts & Drivers

Dimming and non-dimming ballasts and LED drivers from leading brands.

  • 0-10V dimming ballasts
  • Programmed-start & instant-start
  • LED drivers, constant current/voltage
  • Philips Advance, Signify & equivalents
🏭

Fixtures & High-Bay

Complete luminaires for warehouses, facilities, and outdoor areas.

  • LED high-bay & low-bay
  • Troffers & panel fixtures
  • Wall packs & flood lighting
  • Area & parking-lot fixtures
🎛️

Controls & Sensors

Smart controls that extend savings and meet code requirements.

  • Occupancy & vacancy sensors
  • Daylight harvesting controls
  • 0-10V & networked dimming
  • Photocells & timeclocks
🚨

Emergency & Exit

Life-safety lighting that satisfies inspection and code.

  • LED exit signs
  • Emergency battery units
  • Combo exit/emergency
  • Backup drivers & inverters
🔩

Accessories & Hardware

The supporting components that complete a project.

  • Lamp holders & sockets (G13, E26)
  • Mounting hardware & brackets
  • Wire, connectors & tombstones
  • Replacement lenses & covers
